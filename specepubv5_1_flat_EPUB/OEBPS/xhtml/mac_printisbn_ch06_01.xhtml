6. Inline elements

Inline elements that are adjacent to one another and should have a space between them should have the space in the preceding element. For example:

        <p class="noindent" id="fri_9781319065669_6Dmy8pD1dA"><b id="fri_9781319065669_kcilyYM8Ck">An </b><i class="tone-change" id="fri_9781319065669_OgWSpZASgR">example</i>.</p>

This is required due to a limitation in the enhancement process wherein spaces between HTML elements are stripped out.
