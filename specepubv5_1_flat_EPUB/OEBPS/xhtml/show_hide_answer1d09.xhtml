Show/Hide Answer

The show/hide widget is a button for questions already in the ebook (no new assessments), and it replaces upside down answers and answer appendices. By using this widget, text questions are converted to assessments and answers are added using the show/hide functionality. There is no gradebook functionality.

With proper flat ePub coding, show/hide can be automated. However, the widget must be used as designed to automate (full question and answer in widget). The bullet list of show/hide types next can have their base question automatically converted, but the video/hint will be added manually after the conversion.

  • Show/hide with hint

  • Show/hide with video

  • Show/hide with hint and video
