6.3. Abbreviation

When an abbreviation is used in the book, the inline element abbr and the title attribute must be used to provide accessibility technology access to full meaning of an abbreviation. If abbr is present, it must have a title attribute. For example:

        <blockquote class="epigraph" id="fri_9781319065669_RBI8cncXSu" role="doc-epigraph"> <p class="noindent" id="fri_9781319065669_anoXHFPaiV">The <abbr title="World Health Organization" id="fri_9781319065669_HEMCCoXJqL">WHO</abbr> was founded in 1948.</p> </blockquote>
