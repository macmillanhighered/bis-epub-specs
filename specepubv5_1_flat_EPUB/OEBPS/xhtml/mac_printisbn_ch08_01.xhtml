8. Assessments

A question should contain all of its elements. For instance, if an image is part of a question, it should be a child of the corresponding <li> element for the question.

Content editorial may request that back links are included for questions and answers. Answers may have back linking applied to them so that a user can go to the full answers section at the back of the book and click on the answer to return to the question.

Label epub:type ARIA role Description
Answer answer   The response to a question. See end of section or end of chapter material for example
Answers answers   A collection of answers. May appear at end of chapter or end of book when it does.
Assessment assessment   A test, quiz, or other activity that helps measure a student's understanding of what is being taught.
Assessments assessments   A collection of assessments.
Feedback feedback   Helpful instruction to the reader based on the result of an assessment. See assessments.
Fill In The Blank Problem fill-in-the-blank-problem   A problem that requires the reader to input a text answer to complete a sentence, statement or similar.
General Problem general problem   A problem with a free-form solution.
Match Problem match-problem   A problem that requires the reader to match the contents of one list with the corresponding items in another list.
Multiple Choice Problem multiple-choice-problem   A problem with a set of potential answers to choose from ‒ some, all or none of which may be correct.
Practice practice   A review exercise or sample.
Practices practices   A collection of practices.
Question question   A statement that requests information or tests a problem. Typically found paired with one or more answers ‒ or possible answers ‒ in self-assessment problems, question and answer interviews and frequently asked questions, although an answer is not required. See end of section or end of chapter material for example
True False Problem true-false-problem   A problem with either a true or false answer.
