3. Accessibility

The following are the required guidelines specified by the W3C that need to be implemented within the EPUB3 file. Macmillan Learning EPUBs must meet the AA accessibility standards.

The most up-to-date version of W3C EPUB Accessibility Requirements can be found at https://www.w3.org/Submission/epub-a11y.

Accessible EPUB conformance has 3 levels - Discovery enabled, accessible, and optimized. We are currently aiming for “Accessible.”

Reference https://www.w3.org/wiki/WebSchemas/Accessibility for all possible values and use.

All Macmillan titles starting with copyright 2019 have the Benetech Global Certified Accessible credential. It is important to maintain this credential and follow all of the specifications outlined in this document, particularly those related to accessibility.

Information on the credential can be found here: https://benetech.org/our-work/born-accessible/certification/
