1. Overview

The purpose of this document is to provide Macmillan Service Providers detailed guidelines and specifications for the production of our accessible EPUB3 files.

Macmillan Learning EPUBs should meet the following W3C standards:

  • EPUB 3.2 : https://www.w3.org/TR/epub-overview-33/
  • [WCAG 2.0] Level AA conformance : https://www.w3.org/Submission/epub-a11y Accessible EPUB conformance has 3 levels - Discovery enabled, accessible, and optimized. We are currently aiming for "Accessible."
  • EPUB for Education : https://w3c.github.io/publ-cg/education/epub-education.html

The most important components of this EPUB conversion:

  • Adding semantics for common educational publishing components and structures.
  • Including accessibility features to enable compliance with accessibility and educational standards.
  • Enabling the identification of discrete content entities.

It is extremely important to read through this entire guideline before beginning conversion. We have taken great care in defining our most important pieces and information that we require in our files and expect that these guidelines will be followed closely. Contact the Macmillan Learning project manager requesting the EPUB if there are any questions or concerns about these specifications.
